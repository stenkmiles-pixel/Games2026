Hi friend,

Welcome to your very first **Cozy Game Guide** letter — your weekly cozy corner. Each week I'll send you one game worth your evening, a quick tip, and what we're playing. Nothing noisy. Let's ease in. 🌿

**🌿 This week's cozy pick: Fields of Mistria**

If your feed has been full of impossibly pretty pixel-art farms lately, this is why. Fields of Mistria takes the Stardew formula and gives it a lavish, animated coat of paint — a charming valley town, gentle days of farming and fishing, and a cast you'll quickly adore. It's still in Early Access, so it's only getting bigger. A lovely place to lose a quiet evening.

→ Is it worth it? Read our take: https://cozygameguide.com/blog/fields-of-mistria-review/

**💡 A quick tip (Stardew Valley)**

Sitting on a pile of crops? Don't sell them raw. Pop them into **kegs** (for wine and juice) or **preserves jars** (for pickles and jam) first — the processed version sells for two to three times more. A few kegs early on snowball into serious gold.

→ More money tricks: https://cozygameguide.com/blog/stardew-valley-make-money-fast/

**🎮 What we're playing: Hello Kitty Island Adventure**

Don't let the bows fool you — we fell hard for this one. It's shockingly deep, has zero microtransactions, and is the comfiest thing on our Switch right now. Pure serotonin after a long day.

→ Our honest take: https://cozygameguide.com/blog/hello-kitty-island-adventure-worth-it/

That's your cozy corner for this week. Hit reply and tell me what *you're* playing — I read every one.

Warmly,
The Cozy Game Guide team ☕
