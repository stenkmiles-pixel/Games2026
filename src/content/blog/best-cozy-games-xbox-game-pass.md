If you have Xbox Game Pass, you're sitting on a rotating library of cozy games you can play at no extra cost — a brilliant way to sample the genre before buying anything. Here are the **best cozy games on Game Pass** to dive into, with what each one is and why it's worth your time.

> Game Pass's catalog changes regularly — games come and go. Always check the current library before you get attached, and install anything you love "while it's there."

## The cozy highlights

### Coral Island
A gorgeous tropical farming sim where you grow crops, befriend a whole town, and dive to restore an ocean reef. It's warm, generous and one of the best-looking cozy games around — a perfect Game Pass headliner. Here's our [full review](/blog/coral-island-review/).

### Stardew Valley
The genre's gold standard regularly lives on Game Pass, and it's a no-brainer if it's in the catalog. Farming, fishing, mining, romance and festivals add up to hundreds of relaxed hours. Our [beginner's guide](/blog/stardew-valley-beginner-guide/) helps you start.

### Spiritfarer
A beautiful, moving management game about caring for spirits and saying goodbye. It's gentle, hand-drawn and quietly profound — a must-play if it's available. See our [Spiritfarer review](/blog/spiritfarer-review/).

### My Time at Sandrock
A sunny crafting-and-workshop sim packed with story, characters and things to build. If you love the *making* side of cozy games more than the farming, this is the one to install.

## More to look for

### PowerWash Simulator
Oddly meditative grime-blasting with no stakes at all — just the satisfying reveal of a clean surface. Perfect for [unwinding after a long day](/blog/best-relaxing-games-after-work/).

### Unpacking
A zen puzzle about pulling belongings out of boxes and quietly telling a life story through where you place them. Short, soothing and complete.

### Slime Rancher 1 & 2
Bright, breezy first-person ranching where you wrangle adorable, bouncy slimes across a colourful world. Cheerful and easy to lose an afternoon to.

### Lego Bricktales, Wytchwood & friends
Charming, gentle adventures — building dioramas in Lego Bricktales, brewing curious concoctions in Wytchwood — often rotate through the catalog. Always worth a look when you need something low-stress.

## Tips for cozy Game Pass gaming

- **Check the "leaving soon" list.** If a cozy game you want is on its way out, prioritise it before it goes.
- **Cloud streaming counts.** Many of these are playable via the cloud, so you can try them instantly on almost any device.
- **Use it to demo before buying.** Game Pass is a great way to test whether a cozy game clicks for you before you commit to owning it.
