If you have Xbox Game Pass, you're sitting on a rotating library of cozy games you can play at no extra cost. Here are the **best cozy games on Game Pass** to dive into.

> Game Pass's catalog changes regularly — games come and go. Always check the current library before you get attached, and "install while it's there."

## The cozy highlights

### Coral Island
A gorgeous tropical farming sim with diving and a whole town to befriend. One of the best cozy games to arrive on Game Pass — here's our [full review](/blog/coral-island-review/).

### Spiritfarer
A beautiful, moving management game about caring for spirits. A must-play if it's in the catalog. See our [Spiritfarer review](/blog/spiritfarer-review/).

### PowerWash Simulator
Oddly meditative grime-blasting with no stakes at all. Perfect for [unwinding after a long day](/blog/best-relaxing-games-after-work/).

### My Time at Sandrock
A sunny crafting-and-workshop sim packed with story and characters.

## More to look for

### Unpacking
A zen puzzle about unpacking boxes and quietly telling a life story.

### Slime Rancher / Slime Rancher 2
Bright, breezy first-person ranching with adorable slimes.

### Eastward / Wytchwood / Lego Bricktales
Charming, gentle adventures that often rotate through the catalog.

## Tips for cozy Game Pass gaming

- **Check the "leaving soon" list.** If a cozy game you want is on its way out, prioritize it.
- **Cloud streaming counts.** Many of these are playable via cloud, so you can try them instantly.
- **Use it to demo before buying.** Game Pass is a great way to test whether a cozy game clicks for you before committing.
