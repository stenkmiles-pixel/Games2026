## The genuine upgrades

### A Pro Controller
If you play [Stardew Valley](/blog/best-cozy-games-switch/) or Animal Crossing for hours, the Pro Controller is the single best comfort upgrade. It's far kinder to your hands than the Joy-Cons over a long session.

### A microSD card
Cozy games are small, but they add up fast. A roomy microSD card means you'll never have to delete a game to install the next one.

### A good carrying case
Most cozy gaming happens in handheld mode — on the couch, in bed, on a trip. A padded case keeps your Switch (and a few game cards) safe wherever you unwind.
