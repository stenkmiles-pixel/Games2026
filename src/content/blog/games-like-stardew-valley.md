You've cleared the Community Center, married your favorite villager, and reached the bottom of the mines. Now what? The good news: Stardew Valley kicked off a whole wave of wonderful cozy farming sims, and plenty of them scratch the exact same itch.

Here are **12 of the best games like Stardew Valley**, from close cousins to fresh twists on the formula.

## What made Stardew Valley so special?

Before the list, it helps to know *why* you loved it. For most people it's the loop: meaningful daily routines, a town full of characters worth befriending, and steady, satisfying progress with zero pressure. The games below each deliver some combination of that — so pick based on which part you're chasing.

## The best games like Stardew Valley

### 1. Coral Island
The closest "Stardew but bigger and in 3D" experience out there. Farming, ranching, diving, and a lovely town to romance. We loved it — here's our [full Coral Island review](/blog/coral-island-review/).

### 2. Story of Seasons: A Wonderful Life
The series that inspired Stardew in the first place. Slower, more life-focused, and deeply charming.

### 3. Fae Farm
A polished, approachable farming sim with magic and four-player co-op. Brighter and gentler than Stardew, and great on Switch.

### 4. My Time at Sandrock
More crafting-and-workshop than pure farming, set in a sunny desert town. Tons of story, characters, and things to build.

### 5. Sun Haven
Stardew's pixel-art DNA crossed with RPG fantasy — races, magic, and three large towns. Surprisingly deep.

### 6. Wylde Flowers
Farm by day, witch by night, with full voice acting that makes the cast feel like real neighbors.

### 7. Roots of Pacha
A Stone Age take on the genre, with a strong focus on community and discovery instead of money.

### 8. Dinkum
An Australian-outback life sim — equal parts Stardew and Animal Crossing, with a relaxed island to develop.

### 9. Littlewood
You've *already* saved the world; now you just rebuild a cozy town. No stamina bar, low pressure, very chill.

### 10. Potion Permit
Swap farming for chemistry: diagnose townsfolk and brew cures in a charming pixel town.

### 11. Travellers Rest
Run a medieval tavern — brew, cook, and serve. A lovely management spin on the cozy loop.

### 12. Disney Dreamlight Valley
Less farm, more life sim, with a cast of Disney icons. See [is it worth it?](/blog/disney-dreamlight-valley-worth-it/)

## How to choose your next farm

- **Want the most Stardew-like?** Coral Island or Sun Haven.
- **Prefer building and crafting?** My Time at Sandrock.
- **Want to play with friends?** Fae Farm has easy co-op.
- **Want zero pressure?** Littlewood removes the stamina grind entirely.
