Coral Island has a big, lovable cast of romanceable characters — and building a relationship is one of the coziest parts of the game. Here's a friendly guide to **how romance and marriage work**.

New to the island? Start with our [Coral Island tips for beginners](/blog/coral-island-tips/), and see our [review](/blog/coral-island-review/) for an overview.

## How relationships work

Every villager has a **heart meter** that goes up as you build a relationship with them. You raise it by:

- **Talking to them every day** — a quick daily chat adds friendship.
- **Giving gifts** — especially their **loved** gifts (more on that below).
- **Completing their quests and seeing their story events**, which trigger as hearts increase.

As the meter fills, you'll unlock cutscenes that deepen each character's story.

## From friendship to dating to marriage

The path generally goes:

1. **Raise hearts** through daily talks and gifts until you reach the dating threshold.
2. **Start dating** the character you're interested in (you can pursue romance once you've built enough of a bond).
3. **Keep raising hearts to the maximum**, watching their romance cutscenes along the way.
4. **Propose** once you've reached the top of their heart meter and obtained the in-game marriage item. Check the relevant shop and your social menu in-game for the current requirement, as updates occasionally tweak the details.

After marriage, your partner moves in and becomes part of your daily farm life.

## Gift tips to speed things up

- **Learn their loved gifts.** Loved gifts give the biggest friendship boost — and **birthdays multiply it**, so save a loved gift for their special day.
- **Don't over-gift.** There's a limited number of gifts that count per week, so make each one a loved or liked item.
- **Avoid disliked gifts.** Giving something they dislike actually lowers the meter — check the social menu if you're unsure.

## A cozy reminder

There's no rush and no wrong choice — romance in Coral Island is meant to be enjoyed at your own pace. Take your time, enjoy the cutscenes, and pick the character whose story you love most.
