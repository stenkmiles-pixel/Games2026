Perfect stocking-stuffers and "just because" gifts.

- **A great cozy game.** Most of the games on our [best cozy games list](/blog/best-cozy-games-2025/) cost less than a takeout dinner. Stardew Valley is the classic pick and frequently on sale.
- **A warm pair of cozy socks.** Non-negotiable gaming-blanket-and-socks energy.
- **A scented candle.** Set the mood for a long farming session.

## $20–$60: the sweet spot

This is where most great gifts live.

- **A new release they've been eyeing.** Check our [reviews](/category/reviews) to pick a winner.
- **A comfy LED desk lamp** for warm, easy-on-the-eyes evening light.
- **Blue-light glasses** for the late-night players.

## $60 and up: the splurge

For a really special gift.

- **A Nintendo Switch Pro Controller** — far comfier than Joy-Cons for long sessions.
- **A weighted blanket** to make the couch even harder to leave.
- **A year of their favorite game's merch** — plushies and art prints of beloved cozy games are everywhere now.
