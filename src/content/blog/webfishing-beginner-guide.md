WEBFISHING took the cozy community by surprise — a charming, low-key multiplayer game where you fish in a peaceful lake, decorate your character, strum a guitar, and hang out with strangers. Here's a friendly beginner's guide to settling in.

## What is WEBFISHING?
It's a relaxed online fishing game built around *vibing*. There are no stakes, no combat, no pressure — just casting your line, catching fish, chatting in the lobby, and slowly unlocking cosmetics. Think of it as a cozy hangout that happens to involve fishing.

## How fishing works
1. **Cast your line** toward the water.
2. When a fish bites, a little **reeling mini-game** starts — keep the marker on the fish to reel it in.
3. Heavier/rarer fish put up more of a fight; better rods make them easier to land.
4. **Sell your catch** at the shop for money.

## Beginner tips
- **Sell fish and buy a better rod early.** A stronger rod makes catching rarer (more valuable) fish much easier — it snowballs from there.
- **Explore the map.** There's more than one spot to fish, and poking around reveals secrets and nicer views.
- **Spend on cosmetics guilt-free.** Half the fun is customizing your little character — hats, colors, accessories. It's purely for vibes.
- **Try the guitar.** You can actually play music; lobbies often turn into impromptu jam sessions.
- **It's multiplayer — say hi.** The community is famously chill. Join a public lobby or share a code with friends.
- **There's no "winning."** Fish at your own pace. It's designed to be a calm place to exist for a while.

## Why it's so cozy
WEBFISHING nails the feeling of a low-pressure third place — somewhere gentle to log into, do a simple satisfying loop, and enjoy other people's company without any of the usual online-game stress.
