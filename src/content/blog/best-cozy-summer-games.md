Long evenings, warm air, and nowhere you have to be — summer is made for cozy games. Whether you want to dive a coral reef, glide over a sun-drenched lake, or just potter about a beachy island, here are the **best cozy games to play this summer**.

## The best cozy summer games

### 1. Coral Island
The ultimate summer farm. You tend crops on a sunny tropical island, dive to restore the coral reef, and befriend a warm, diverse town. It's gorgeous, generous, and practically radiates sunshine. See our [full review](/blog/coral-island-review/).

### 2. Dave the Diver
Dive a beautiful blue ocean by day, run a sushi bar by night. It's not *strictly* a cozy game, but it's charming, low-stress, and the perfect "one more dive" companion for a hot afternoon.

### 3. A Short Hike
Climb a sun-dappled mountain, glide on warm updrafts, and chat with friendly critters around a lake. A couple of hours of pure summer warmth — and one of the best [short cozy games](/blog/best-short-cozy-games/) there is.

### 4. Animal Crossing: New Horizons
Summer is when New Horizons shines: swimming and diving for sea creatures, catching beetles at night, and watching fireworks shows in August. Island life has never felt so seasonal.

### 5. Wylde Flowers
A cozy island farming sim with witchy magic and full voice acting that makes the seaside town feel alive. Lovely and warm from start to finish — here's our [Wylde Flowers review](/blog/wylde-flowers-review/).

### 6. WEBFISHING
A wonderfully chill multiplayer fishing hangout — cast a line, vibe with strangers, and decorate your little space. Perfect, low-effort summer comfort. New? See our [WEBFISHING beginner's guide](/blog/webfishing-beginner-guide/).

### 7. Hello Kitty Island Adventure
A sunny, generous open-world island packed with Sanrio charm, beaches to explore, and zero microtransactions. Surprisingly deep summer comfort — we cover it in [is it worth it?](/blog/hello-kitty-island-adventure-worth-it/)

### 8. Spiritfarer
A gentle voyage across a glittering sea, caring for spirit passengers aboard your boat. Beautiful, calming, and quietly profound — ideal for a warm, reflective evening. Read our [Spiritfarer review](/blog/spiritfarer-review/).

## How to pick your summer game

- **Want the ocean?** Coral Island, Dave the Diver, or our [best cozy fishing games](/blog/best-cozy-fishing-games/).
- **Only have an evening?** A Short Hike or Spiritfarer.
- **Playing with friends?** WEBFISHING or Hello Kitty Island Adventure.
