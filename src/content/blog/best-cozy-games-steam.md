Steam is a goldmine for cozy games — and with regular sales, free demos, and a handy wishlist, it's the cheapest place to build a relaxing library. Here are the **best cozy games on Steam** to wishlist (and play) right now.

## The must-plays

### Stardew Valley
The cornerstone of the genre and a Steam evergreen. Endless depth, mod support, and it goes on sale constantly. New? Start with our [beginner's guide](/blog/stardew-valley-beginner-guide/).

### Coral Island
A gorgeous 3D farming sim with diving and ocean restoration. Read our [full review](/blog/coral-island-review/) — and try the free demo on its Steam page first.

### Spiritfarer
A beautiful management game about caring for spirits. Hand-drawn, moving, and deeply relaxing. See our [Spiritfarer review](/blog/spiritfarer-review/).

## Gentle and low-key

### Dorfromantik
A serene tile-laying builder — pure, wordless calm. Perfect for a stressful day.

### Unpacking
A zen puzzle about unpacking boxes and telling a life story through objects.

### Slime Rancher 2
Bright, breezy first-person ranching with adorable slimes to wrangle.

## More to do

### My Time at Sandrock
A sunny crafting-and-workshop sim with tons of story and characters.

### Wylde Flowers
Farming with a witchy twist and full voice acting. Our [Wylde Flowers review](/blog/wylde-flowers-review/) has the details.

### Palia
A free-to-play cozy MMO, right on Steam — farming, fishing and decorating with friends.

## How to play cozy games cheaply on Steam

Steam makes it easy to spend less:

- **Wishlist everything.** You'll get an email the moment a wishlisted game hits a sale.
- **Wait for the big seasonal sales** (Summer, Autumn, Winter). Cozy games are discounted heavily.
- **Always try the demo.** Many cozy games offer free demos — the best way to test performance and vibe before buying.
- **Check "Next Fest."** Steam's regular demo festivals are full of upcoming cozy gems.
