There's something deeply soothing about fishing in games — the quiet water, the gentle wait, the little thrill of a catch. Here are the **best cozy fishing games**, whether you want peaceful solo casts or a silly fishing hangout with friends.

## Fishing as the main event

### WEBFISHING
A delightfully chill multiplayer game where you fish in a cozy lake, chat with strangers, and just vibe. A surprise hit and one of the most relaxing online hangouts around.

### Moonglow Bay
A slice-of-life fishing RPG with a heartfelt story and a charming voxel art style. Fishing meets cozy narrative.

### Dave the Diver
Part relaxing ocean explorer, part sushi-restaurant manager. More involved than most, but endlessly charming and hard to put down.

## Great fishing inside cozy sims

### Stardew Valley
Fishing is one of the best parts of Stardew — perfect on rainy days, and a great early money-maker. See our [money guide](/blog/stardew-valley-make-money-fast/).

### Coral Island
Beautiful fishing *and* diving to restore the reef. Our [Coral Island review](/blog/coral-island-review/) has the full picture.

### Spiritfarer
Casting a line off your boat at sunset is peak cozy. One of many gentle activities in our [reviewed](/blog/spiritfarer-review/) favorite.

### Animal Crossing: New Horizons
Catch fish (and bugs) to fill your museum and earn Bells. Classic, low-pressure fun on [Switch](/blog/best-cozy-games-switch/).

## What makes fishing "cozy"

The best cozy fishing isn't twitchy or punishing — it's about the calm rhythm of casting, waiting, and reeling in something new for your collection. No leaderboards, no stress, just you and the water.
