## 1. Get the lighting right (the biggest difference)
Harsh white light kills the cozy mood. Swap it for **warm, soft, layered lighting**:

- A **warm-toned LED desk or table lamp** (look for "warm white / 2700K")
- **Fairy lights** or a small **LED strip** behind your monitor for a gentle glow
- A **candle** (or flameless candle) for instant atmosphere

## 2. Make seating comfy
You'll be here a while, so be comfortable:

- A **soft, supportive chair** — or a beanbag/floor cushion for handheld play
- A **weighted or chunky-knit blanket** (non-negotiable, honestly)
- A couple of **soft cushions**

## 3. Soft textures & little friends
Cozy is about texture and personality:

- A **plushie or two** of your favorite cozy game characters
- A small **rug** to warm up the floor
- A **mug warmer** so your tea or coffee stays hot through a long session

## 4. A touch of green & calm
- A **low-maintenance plant** (pothos, snake plant) or a cute faux plant
- A small **diffuser** or incense for a gentle scent
- **Lo-fi or game soundtracks** playing softly in the background
