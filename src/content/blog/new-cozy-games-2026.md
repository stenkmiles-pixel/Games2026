The cozy genre keeps getting bigger, and 2026 is shaping up to be a wonderful year for relaxing games. Here are the **new and upcoming cozy games to watch** — a mix of fresh releases and hotly anticipated titles.

> Release dates in this space shift often. Treat this as a watchlist, and wishlist anything that catches your eye so you hear the moment it lands.

## Most anticipated

### Tales of the Shire
A cozy life sim set in the world of *The Lord of the Rings*, where you live the gentle hobbit life in Bywater — cooking, gardening, foraging, and befriending neighbors. About as cozy as a premise gets.

### Witchbrook
A long-awaited magic-school life sim from Chucklefish (the studio behind Stardew's release). Think cozy farming-sim systems crossed with attending a school of witchcraft.

### Hello Kitty Island Adventure
A charming, content-packed life sim (originally an Apple Arcade hit) expanding to more platforms — exploration, decorating, and friendship with Sanrio characters.

## Rising stars to watch

### Fields of Mistria
A stunning pixel-art farming sim that took the community by storm in early access. If you love [Stardew Valley](/blog/games-like-stardew-valley/), this is one to follow closely.

### Loftia
A cozy social MMO with farming, decorating, and a wholesome community focus — one of the most promising upcoming cozy multiplayer games.

### Wanderstop
A cozy game about running a tea shop, from a celebrated developer. A gentle, reflective experience with a lot of heart.

## How to keep up with new cozy releases

- **Wishlist on Steam.** You'll get an email the moment a game launches or hits a sale. See our [best cozy games on Steam](/blog/best-cozy-games-steam/) for how to make the most of it.
- **Watch Steam Next Fest.** These demo festivals are full of upcoming cozy gems you can try for free.
- **Follow the cozy community.** Cozy-game showcases and creators are the best early-warning system for the next big thing.
