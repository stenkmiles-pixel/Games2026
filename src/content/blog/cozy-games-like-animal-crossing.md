Animal Crossing: New Horizons is a one-of-a-kind comfort blanket — but it's Switch-only, and sometimes you just want a fresh island to potter around. Here are **10 cozy games like Animal Crossing**, full of decorating, friendly neighbours, and that signature low-pressure charm, across every platform.

## The closest in spirit

### Disney Dreamlight Valley
A life sim with deep decorating, cooking, fishing and a cast of beloved Disney and Pixar characters to befriend — and it's on nearly every platform, including phones. It's the closest thing to that daily Animal Crossing rhythm off the Switch. See [is it worth it?](/blog/disney-dreamlight-valley-worth-it/)

### Hokko Life
A community-building sim where you design your own furniture from scratch, decorate, and shape a cosy town to your taste. If your favourite part of Animal Crossing is the *creativity*, its deep customisation will delight you.

### Dinkum
An Australian-outback life sim that's equal parts Animal Crossing and [Stardew Valley](/blog/games-like-stardew-valley/) — you develop a relaxed island town, catch critters, farm, and befriend visitors, with co-op for friends.

### Palia
A free-to-play cosy MMO with decorating, gathering and villagers to befriend in a gentle open world you share with other players. It's the easiest no-cost way to scratch the itch — and we weigh it up in [is Palia worth playing?](/blog/is-palia-worth-playing-2026/)

## More lovely alternatives

### Garden Paws
Run a shop and farm as adorable animal characters, with co-op so friends can join. It's open-ended and charming, with that same "do a little of everything" freedom.

### Go-Go Town
A bright, playful town-management sim about reviving a quirky little town — part Animal Crossing decorating, part light management, with a great sense of humour.

### Cozy Grove
Designed for short daily sessions on a haunted-but-adorable island, it's the perfect "morning coffee" game — a few gentle tasks each day, Animal Crossing style, with a hand-drawn storybook look.

### Wylde Flowers
Less island, more seaside village, but the warm community, decorating and daily rhythm scratch the same itch — with a heartfelt, fully voiced story and a dash of witchcraft. Read our [review](/blog/wylde-flowers-review/).

### Spiritea
A cosy game blending tea-making, spirit-collecting and town life in a charming, lantern-lit world. A lovely, lower-profile pick for fans of gentle daily loops.

### Building sandboxes
For the pure "build and decorate" urge between sessions, gentle building games like [Tiny Glade](/blog/tiny-glade-review/) let you create pretty little scenes with zero pressure.

## How to choose

- **Want it on a console that isn't Switch?** Disney Dreamlight Valley or Dinkum.
- **Want it free?** Palia, or see our [best free cozy games](/blog/best-free-cozy-games/).
- **Want decorating above all?** Hokko Life or a building sandbox.
