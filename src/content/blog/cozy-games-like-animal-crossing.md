Animal Crossing: New Horizons is a one-of-a-kind comfort blanket — but it's Switch-only, and sometimes you want a fresh island to potter around. Here are **10 cozy games like Animal Crossing**, full of decorating, friendly neighbors, and that signature low-pressure charm.

## The closest in spirit

### Disney Dreamlight Valley
A life sim with decorating, cooking, and a cast of beloved characters — and it's on nearly every platform. See [is it worth it?](/blog/disney-dreamlight-valley-worth-it/)

### Hokko Life
A community-building sim where you design furniture, decorate, and shape a cozy town. Wonderfully customizable.

### Dinkum
An Australian-outback life sim — equal parts Animal Crossing and [Stardew Valley](/blog/games-like-stardew-valley/), with a relaxed island to develop.

### Palia
A free-to-play cozy MMO with decorating, gathering, and villagers to befriend. New here? See our [Palia beginner's guide](/blog/palia-beginner-guide/).

## More lovely alternatives

### Garden Paws
Run a shop and farm as adorable animal characters, with co-op for friends.

### Go-Go Town
A bright, playful town-management sim about reviving a quirky little town.

### Spiritea
A cozy game blending tea-making, spirit-collecting, and town life in a charming world.

### Cozy Grove
Designed for short daily sessions on a haunted-but-adorable island — perfect "morning coffee" play.

### Wylde Flowers
Less island, more village, but the warm community and decorating scratch the same itch — with full voice acting. Read our [review](/blog/wylde-flowers-review/).

### Lego Fortnite / building sandboxes
For the pure "build and decorate" urge, gentle building sandboxes can fill the gap between Animal Crossing sessions.

## How to choose

- **Want it on a console that isn't Switch?** Disney Dreamlight Valley or Dinkum.
- **Want it free?** Palia, or see our [best free cozy games](/blog/best-free-cozy-games/).
- **Want decorating above all?** Hokko Life.
