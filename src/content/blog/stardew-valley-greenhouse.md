The greenhouse lets you grow crops **year-round**, regardless of season — making it one of the most valuable upgrades in Stardew Valley. Here's exactly how to unlock it, by either path.

New here? See our [beginner's guide](/blog/stardew-valley-beginner-guide/) and [Community Center guide](/blog/stardew-valley-community-center-guide/).

## The greenhouse sits on your farm — but broken
From day one there's a ruined greenhouse on your farm. You can't use it until it's repaired, and there are two ways to make that happen.

## Path 1: Community Center (the cozy route)
The greenhouse is the reward for completing the **Pantry** room bundles in the Community Center. The Pantry bundles ask for **farming and animal products** — crops across all seasons, plus things like milk, eggs, and artisan goods.

Tips to finish the Pantry faster:
- **Keep one of each crop** you grow (don't sell your only sample) — see the [best crops guide](/blog/stardew-valley-best-crops/).
- Some bundles want **specific-season crops**, so check what's needed before a season ends.
- Animal products (large milk, large egg) come once you've bought a coop and barn.

Complete the Pantry and the Junimos repair your greenhouse for free. This is the recommended path.

## Path 2: Joja (the money route)
If you bought a **Joja membership**, the Community Center is replaced by the Joja Community Development Form. There you can **purchase the greenhouse for 35,000g**. Faster if you're swimming in gold, but it costs money and skips the cozy Junimo story.

## Why the greenhouse is so good
- **Grow any crop in any season**, including winter.
- Perfect home for **multi-harvest, high-value crops** like [Ancient Fruit](/blog/stardew-valley-ancient-fruit/) — plant once, harvest forever, turn into wine for huge profit.
- A reliable income engine that runs all year.

## After you unlock it
Fill it with your most profitable regrowable crop and pair it with kegs. Combined with the tips in our [money guide](/blog/stardew-valley-make-money-fast/), your greenhouse becomes a year-round gold machine. 🌿
