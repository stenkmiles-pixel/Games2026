Disney Dreamlight Valley is wonderfully welcoming, but there's a lot to juggle early on — friendships, cooking, gardening, mining, and clearing all that Night Thorn. Here are **12 beginner tips** to help you start strong.

Wondering if the game's for you first? Read [is Disney Dreamlight Valley worth it?](/blog/disney-dreamlight-valley-worth-it/)

## Money & resources

### 1. Cook meals to make money fast
Selling cooked meals is one of the best early income sources — far better than selling raw ingredients. Learn a few high-value recipes and cook in bulk.

### 2. Sell your surplus crops
Plant fast-growing, profitable crops, then sell the extras. Reinvest into bigger harvests to snowball your Star Coins.

### 3. Clear Night Thorns daily
Removing the purple Night Thorns drops resources and Dreamlight, and tidies up your valley. Clear them as you pass.

### 4. Don't ignore Dreamlight
Dreamlight (the secondary currency) unlocks new biomes and realms. Complete the simple Dreamlight Duties tasks to earn it steadily.

## Friendships

### 5. Level up friendships for perks
Hanging out with and gifting characters raises their friendship level, which unlocks helpful perks and story quests. Prioritize a few favorites early.

### 6. Give daily gifts
Each character has favored gifts that rotate daily (shown by a heart icon). Daily gifting is the fastest way to build friendships.

### 7. Assign a friend to help you
Bring a high-level companion along on a "role" (mining, gardening, fishing, digging) for bonus resources while you work.

## Daily routine

### 8. Visit Scrooge's store every day
Scrooge's shop restocks daily with furniture and items — check it for things you can't get elsewhere.

### 9. Fish the glowing ripples
Circles of bubbles in the water yield rarer fish worth more coins. Always prioritize them.

### 10. Plan around energy and time
There's no harsh fail state, but a tidy daily loop — water crops, clear thorns, gift friends, cook — keeps progress steady.

## Unlocking the valley

### 11. Save Dreamlight for biomes you need
Some unlocks gate important resources and characters. Spend Dreamlight on the areas that move your current quests forward.

### 12. Follow the quests
The character and Dreamlight quests are your roadmap. When in doubt, open your quest log and chase the next objective.
