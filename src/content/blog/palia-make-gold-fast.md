Gold is what funds your home, tools, and upgrades in Palia. The good news: because Palia is all about gathering and crafting, making gold is relaxing rather than grindy. Here's **how to make gold fast** as a beginner.

New to the game? Start with our [Palia beginner's guide](/blog/palia-beginner-guide/).

## The core idea: gather, craft, sell

In Palia, raw materials sell for a little, but **crafted and processed goods sell for more**. The loop is simple:

1. Gather lots of materials (foraging, mining, fishing, bug catching, hunting).
2. Process or craft them into higher-value items.
3. Sell to the right vendor.

## Best ways to earn

### Sell what you don't need
You'll naturally gather more than you use. Sell surplus materials and crafted goods regularly instead of letting them pile up in storage.

### Focus on high-value skills
As you level your gathering skills, you unlock higher-tier resources that sell for much more. Lean into the skills you enjoy — fishing and hunting can be especially lucrative once leveled.

### Craft for profit
Furniture and processed goods generally sell for more than raw materials. Set up crafting stations on your plot and keep them running while you do other things.

### Cook and sell meals
Like many cozy games, cooked dishes can be worth more than their ingredients — a tidy way to turn a garden harvest into gold.

## Daily & weekly habits

- **Do the daily/weekly activities** — they hand out useful rewards and materials.
- **Join group hunts and events** — community activities are an easy, fun source of valuable drops.
- **Keep your garden growing** — passive crops mean a steady stream of sellable produce.

## A cozy reminder

Palia rewards a relaxed, steady pace — you don't need to min-max. Gather what you enjoy, sell the extra, and the gold adds up naturally.
