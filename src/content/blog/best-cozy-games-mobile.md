Your phone is the ultimate cozy gaming machine — always in your pocket, perfect for a few relaxing minutes anywhere. Here are the **best cozy games on mobile** for iOS and Android.

## Full-fat cozy games on the go

### Stardew Valley
The complete farming sim, fully playable on your phone with touch controls and cloud-friendly saves. Astonishing value for the price. Our [beginner's guide](/blog/stardew-valley-beginner-guide/) applies here too.

### Disney Dreamlight Valley
The Disney life sim has a mobile version, so you can tend your valley from anywhere. See [is it worth it?](/blog/disney-dreamlight-valley-worth-it/)

### Cozy Grove
Designed for short daily sessions, which makes it perfect for mobile. A few minutes of ghostly bear tasks each day.

## Made for the phone

### Tsuki's Odyssey
A gorgeous idle life sim set in a peaceful village. It gently ticks along whether you're watching or not.

### Alba: A Wildlife Adventure
Explore a sunny Mediterranean island, photograph animals, and make a small difference. Warm and wholesome.

### Good Pizza, Great Pizza
A surprisingly relaxing pizza-making sim with a charming hand-drawn style.

## Lovely idle & casual picks

### Animal Restaurant
A cozy idle game about running a restaurant staffed by cats. Deep and very chill.

### Neko Atsume
The classic: put out food and toys, then collect the adorable cats that visit.

### Two Dots
A beautiful, minimalist puzzle game that's endlessly soothing in small doses.

## Tips for cozy mobile gaming

- **Mind the monetization.** Free mobile games often have ads or in-app purchases — paid games like Stardew Valley are usually a better cozy experience with no interruptions.
- **Turn on Do Not Disturb** for a properly peaceful session.
- **Look at Apple Arcade / Google Play Pass.** These subscriptions bundle several ad-free cozy games.
