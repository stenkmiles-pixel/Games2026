The PS5 might be known for blockbusters, but it's a lovely home for cozy games too. Here are the **best cozy games on PS5** to relax with.

## The essentials

### Stardew Valley
The cornerstone of the genre, and it runs beautifully on PS5. New? Grab our [beginner's guide](/blog/stardew-valley-beginner-guide/).

### Coral Island
A stunning 3D farming sim with diving and ocean restoration — a perfect showcase for the PS5. Read the [review](/blog/coral-island-review/).

### Disney Dreamlight Valley
The Disney life sim, fully featured on PS5. See [is it worth it?](/blog/disney-dreamlight-valley-worth-it/)

### Spiritfarer
A gorgeous, moving management game about caring for spirits. Plays wonderfully on PlayStation.

## More cozy PS5 picks

### Fae Farm
A polished, approachable farming sim with co-op — compare it in [Fae Farm vs Stardew Valley](/blog/fae-farm-vs-stardew-valley/).

### Story of Seasons: A Wonderful Life
A warm farming-and-family classic, right at home on PS5.

### Unpacking
A zen puzzle about unpacking boxes and arranging a home.

### PowerWash Simulator
Soothing, stakes-free grime-blasting — ideal for [decompressing after work](/blog/best-relaxing-games-after-work/).

### Slime Rancher 2
Bright, breezy first-person ranching with adorable slimes.

## A quick note on Animal Crossing

One of the most-asked questions: **is Animal Crossing on PS5?** No — Animal Crossing is a Nintendo exclusive and only available on Switch. If that's the vibe you want on PlayStation, start with **Disney Dreamlight Valley** or **Dinkum**, or see our full list of [games like Animal Crossing](/blog/cozy-games-like-animal-crossing/).

For picks on other platforms, see the [best cozy games on Switch](/blog/best-cozy-games-switch/) and [Steam](/blog/best-cozy-games-steam/). 🎮
