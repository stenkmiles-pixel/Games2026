Gifts are the fastest way to build friendships (and romance) in Stardew Valley. Give someone a *loved* gift and you'll earn 80 friendship points — and double that on their birthday. Give the wrong thing and you'll actually lose points.

This guide covers the **universal loved gifts** that work on almost everyone, plus the best **loved gifts for each marriage candidate**.

## Universal loved gifts (almost everyone loves these)

These are loved by nearly every villager, making them the safest gifts in the game:

- **Prismatic Shard**
- **Pearl**
- **Magic Rock Candy**
- **Rabbit's Foot**
- **Golden Pumpkin**

The catch: they're rare or valuable. Save them for villagers you most want to befriend quickly — or for birthdays, when the friendship boost is doubled.

## Universal likes (good fallback gifts)

When you don't know someone's tastes, these are usually safe:

- Most **flowers** (like Tulips and Sunflowers)
- Most **fruit** and many **cooked dishes**
- **Gold Bars**, **Maple Syrup**, and most **gems**

## Universal hates (never gift these!)

Avoid giving anyone:

- **Trash**, **Broken items**, and **Driftwood**
- **Bait**, **Tackle**, and **Crafting materials** (most people dislike these)

## Smart gifting tips

- **You can gift twice per week** per villager (plus their birthday). Make those gifts count.
- **Birthdays give 8x the friendship.** Check the calendar in Pierre's shop and save a loved gift for the day.
- **Wine and gems are versatile.** Wine is loved or liked by many adults, and a spare Diamond pleases lots of villagers.
- **Don't waste universal loves early.** A Pumpkin works just as well on Abigail as a Prismatic Shard — save the rare stuff.
