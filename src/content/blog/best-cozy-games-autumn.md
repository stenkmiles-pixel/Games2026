Autumn and cozy games are a perfect match — pumpkins, falling leaves, warm drinks, and early dark evenings practically beg for a blanket and a gentle game. Here are the **best cozy games to play in autumn**.

## Pure sweater-weather vibes

### Stardew Valley
Fall is arguably Stardew's coziest season — cranberries, pumpkins, the Spirit's Eve festival, and golden trees everywhere. The perfect autumn farm. New? See our [beginner's guide](/blog/stardew-valley-beginner-guide/).

### Cozy Grove
Autumnal by design — a haunted-but-adorable island in eternal fall, played in calm daily sessions. Peak spooky-cozy.

### Spiritfarer
Gentle, reflective, and gorgeous at dusk. A wonderful game to sink into on a dark, rainy evening — here's our [review](/blog/spiritfarer-review/).

## Rainy-day comfort

### Coffee Talk
Brew warm drinks and chat with customers as the rain falls outside. It's basically autumn in game form.

### Animal Crossing: New Horizons
Fall brings mushrooms, acorns, falling leaves, and Halloween events to your island. Lovely seasonal decorating on [Switch](/blog/best-cozy-games-switch/).
