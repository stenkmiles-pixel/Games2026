Love a spooky atmosphere but hate jump scares? "Cozy horror" is the sweet spot — games with witchy, autumnal, slightly macabre vibes that stay gentle and relaxing. Here are the **best cozy horror games** for spooky season (or any night you want a little atmosphere).

## Gentle and ghostly

### Cozy Grove
You're a spirit scout on a haunted island helping friendly ghost bears. Spooky-adorable, designed for calm daily sessions — about as gentle as "horror" gets.

### Spiritfarer
A beautiful management game about ferrying spirits to the afterlife. The theme is death, but the tone is warm, moving, and peaceful — see our [review](/blog/spiritfarer-review/).

### Strange Horticulture
An eerie, atmospheric puzzle game about identifying plants in a mysterious occult shop. Cozy, tactile, and just unsettling enough.

## Witchy & macabre management

### Graveyard Keeper
A medieval graveyard-management sim with a dark sense of humor. It's a cozy build-and-manage loop wrapped in gravestones and questionable ethics.

### Wytchwood
A gothic-fairytale crafting adventure about gathering ingredients and brewing curious concoctions. Spooky storybook charm.

### Cult of the Lamb
Run an adorable cult: build your flock's village, decorate, and manage routines (with some light roguelike combat). Cute-creepy and surprisingly cozy between runs.

## Atmospheric & narrative

### Night in the Woods
More melancholy than scary — a beautiful, moody story about coming home to a fading town. Autumnal and atmospheric.

## A note on "horror"
None of these are true scream-in-the-dark horror. They borrow the *aesthetic* — pumpkins, ghosts, witches, fog — while keeping the gameplay gentle. If you want spooky-season vibes without the stress, this is your list.
