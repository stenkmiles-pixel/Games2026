Cooking is the fastest, most reliable way to earn Star Coins in Disney Dreamlight Valley. The trick is cooking **high-value meals from cheap, easy ingredients**. Here are the best money recipes and how to use them.

New here? See our [beginner tips](/blog/disney-dreamlight-valley-tips/) and [is it worth it?](/blog/disney-dreamlight-valley-worth-it/)

## Why cooking beats selling raw ingredients

A cooked dish almost always sells for more than the sum of its ingredients — and the more ingredients (and rarer ones) a dish uses, the higher its value. Cook in bulk, sell the stack, repeat.

## Reliable money-makers

The exact best dish shifts with updates, but these categories consistently pay well:

- **Five-ingredient entrées** — complex dishes (think hearty fish or meat dinners with multiple components) sell for the most. Grow or buy the ingredients cheaply, cook, and profit.
- **Seafood dishes** — fish are easy to catch for free, so meals built around them have great margins.
- **Crop-based meals** — when you grow your own vegetables in bulk, crop-heavy dishes turn near-free ingredients into solid Star Coins.

> Tip: the in-game recipe book shows each dish's sell value. Sort by value and look for the high earners you can make cheaply.

## Maximize your profit

- **Grow your own ingredients.** Buy seeds, plant in bulk, and your cooking costs drop to almost nothing.
- **Fish the glowing ripples** for free, valuable seafood.
- **Cook in big batches** — make 10+ of a profitable dish at once, then sell the lot to Goofy's stalls or any shop.
- **Level up friendships** with characters who can join you as a **gardening or fishing companion** for bonus ingredients.

## Don't forget meals have other uses

High-energy meals also refill your character's energy and make great gifts for villagers — so cooking pulls double duty.
