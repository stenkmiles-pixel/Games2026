There's something perfect about a cozy game in handheld mode — curled up under a blanket, screen glowing softly, no pressure in sight. The Nintendo Switch might be the best cozy gaming machine ever made, and its library is overflowing.

Here are the **best cozy games on Nintendo Switch** to sink into right now.

## The essentials

### Animal Crossing: New Horizons
The definitive Switch cozy game. Build your island, decorate to your heart's content, and let real-world time drift by. If you own a Switch, this is non-negotiable.

### Stardew Valley
Runs beautifully in handheld and feels like it was made for it. The complete farming-sim package — new players should grab our [beginner's guide](/blog/stardew-valley-beginner-guide/).

### Spiritfarer
A gorgeous, gentle management game about caring for spirits before they move on. Plays wonderfully on the go.

## Lovely and low-key

### Dorfromantik
A serene tile-laying puzzle that's ideal for handheld — place hexes, grow a peaceful landscape, breathe out.

### A Short Hike
A couple of hours of pure joy: climb, glide, and chat with friendly critters. Perfect for a single cozy evening.

### Unpacking
Unpack boxes, arrange a home, and uncover a quiet life story. Strangely moving and totally stress-free.

### Cozy Grove
Designed for short daily sessions — a few minutes of ghostly bear tasks each day. Great as a "morning coffee" game.

## Cozy with a little more to do

### Fae Farm
A polished farming sim with magic and four-player co-op, built with Switch in mind.

### Story of Seasons: A Wonderful Life
A warm farming-and-family classic, remade and right at home on Switch.

### Little Kitty, Big City
Be a cat. Knock things off shelves. Befriend the neighborhood. Short, funny, and adorable.

### Wylde Flowers
Fully voice-acted farming with a witchy twist that makes the town come alive.

## Tips for cozy Switch gaming

- **Handheld is the cozy way.** Most of these shine in handheld mode — easier on the eyes at night than a big TV.
