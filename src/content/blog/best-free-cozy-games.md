Cozy games are comfort food — and the best news is that plenty of them are completely free. Whether you're between paychecks or just want to try the genre risk-free, here are the **best free cozy games** you can start playing today.

## Free on PC

### Palia
A genuinely free-to-play cozy MMO: farming, fishing, cooking, decorating, and exploring a beautiful world alongside other players (or happily solo). The biggest free cozy game going.

### Window Garden
A free, lo-fi desktop game about growing a little plant collection while you work. Pure background calm.

### Sort the Court!
A charming free kingdom-management game — answer yes/no to your subjects and watch your realm grow.

## Free on mobile

### Tsuki's Odyssey
A gorgeous idle life sim set in a peaceful village. It ticks along whether you're watching or not.

### Neko Atsume
The classic cat-collecting game. Put out food and toys, then wait for adorable cats to visit.

### Animal Restaurant
A cozy idle game about running a restaurant staffed by cats. Relaxing and surprisingly deep.

## How to play premium cozy games for free (or nearly)

Some of the best cozy games aren't free — but you can still play them for very little:

- **Free demos.** Tons of cozy games (including [Coral Island](/blog/coral-island-review/)) offer free Steam demos. A great way to try before you buy.
- **Game subscriptions.** Services like Game Pass and Apple Arcade regularly include cozy titles at no extra cost. Wylde Flowers, for instance, launched on Apple Arcade.
- **Free prologues.** Many developers release a free "prologue" chapter on Steam to hook you.
- **Seasonal sales.** Cozy games are discounted constantly — wishlist them and wait.

## A note on "free-to-play"

Free-to-play games like Palia make money through optional cosmetics. None of our picks above require you to spend a cent to enjoy them — but always check what's optional versus required before diving in.
